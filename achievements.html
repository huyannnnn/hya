Achievements of FC Barcelona

Major Titles

  • La Liga: 26 titles
  • Copa del Rey: 31 titles
  • UEFA Champions League: 5 titles
  • UEFA Super Cup: 5 titles
  • FIFA Club World Cup: 3 titles

Individual Achievements

FC Barcelona players have won numerous Ballon d'Or awards, with stars like Lionel Messi setting records as the greatest player in history. The club's youth academy, La Masia, continues to produce world-class talent.

Legacy of Excellence

From its historic victories to its role in shaping modern football, FC Barcelona remains at the pinnacle of the sport. The team's influence goes beyond titles, showcasing football artistry and innovation.
