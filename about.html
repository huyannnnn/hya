About FC Barcelona

More Than a Club

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Known as "Barça," the club is a symbol of Catalan culture and identity. The famous motto, "Més que un club" ("More than a club"), embodies its commitment to social responsibility and community engagement beyond football.

Why FC Barcelona Stands Out

  • A world-renowned youth academy, La Masia, producing football stars.
  • Success in multiple competitions, including La Liga, Copa del Rey, and UEFA Champions League.
  • A passionate fan base that fills the iconic Camp Nou stadium.
